The PAWSOME Friends of Paw Patrol!

Ryder is a 10-year-old boy and the leader of the PAW Patrol. He was voiced by Owen Mason[5] from Season 1-Mid Season 2, by Elijha Hammill from Late Season 2-Mid Season 3, Jaxon Mercey from Late Season 3-Mid Season 6, and by Joey Nijem from late-Season 6 onward

Marshall is a clumsy but competent Dalmatian who serves as a firefighter and paramedic dog. He was voiced by Gage Munroe from Season 1, by Drew Davis from Season 2-season 5, by Lukas Engel from 2019 to 2020, and by Kingsley Marshall from 2020 onward.

Chase is a German Shepherd serves as a police and spy dog, who also has a cat and feather allergy. He was voiced by Tristan Samuel from Season 1, by Max Calinescu from Season 2-Season 4, and by Justin Paul Kelly from Season 5 onward.

Rubble is a bulldog who serves as a construction dog. He is voiced by Devan Cohen from 2013 through 2019, and by Keegan Hedley from 2019 onward.

Rocky is a grey-and-white mixed-breed pup who serves as a recycling and handyman dog. He was voiced by Stuart Ralston from 2013 to 2015 and by Samuel Faraci from 2015 onward.

Zuma is a chocolate Labrador retriever who serves as an aquatic rescue pup. He was voiced by Alex Thorne from Season 1-Season3 Carter Thorne from Season 4-Season 5, and by Shayle Simons from 2019 onward.

Skye is a cockapoo who serves as an aviator. She is voiced by Kallan Holley from 2013 through 2019, and by Lilly Bartlam from 2019 onward.
